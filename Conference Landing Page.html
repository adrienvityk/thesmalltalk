What is The Small Talk??
The Small Talk is an engaging conference where ideas, experiences, and expertise come to life. Each edition brings together inspiring speakers, experts, professionals, and passionate individuals, who share powerful insights on a common theme. Through thought-provoking talks, The Small Talk sparks meaningful discussions, broadens perspectives, and connects people through knowledge and storytelling.

More than just a conference, it’s a space for curiosity, learning, and inspiration, designed for those who love to explore new ideas and see the world from different angles.
THIS EDITION IS:
Meet Me Beyond the Border
In a world that is more connected than ever, understanding and embracing different cultures has become essential. The Small Talk: Meet Me Beyond the Border is a conference dedicated to intercultural awareness and the transformative power of travel. Through engaging talks from experts and passionate storytellers, this event explores how crossing borders, both physical and cultural, shapes our perspectives, challenges our assumptions, and fosters deeper connections.

Whether through personal journeys, professional experiences, or academic insights, our speakers will share unique perspectives on what it means to truly engage with the world beyond our own. Small Talk is an invitation to explore, learn, and embrace the richness of diversity. Join us for an inspiring evening where ideas travel as far as we do.
